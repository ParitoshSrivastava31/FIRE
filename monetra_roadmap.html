Product Vision

India's First Lifestyle-to-
Investment AI Planner

Monetra bridges the gap between how Indians live and how they invest — powered by real-time AI intelligence.

The gap is not awareness — it's actionability. India has 500M+ working adults, yet only ~3% invest in mutual funds and less than 1% in equities. Existing tools either track expenses OR suggest investments, but none bridge the two with real-time AI intelligence tailored to India-specific instruments, regional real estate data, and behavioural nudges.

Core Value Propositions

01
Lifestyle-to-Investment Bridge
Connect spending habits directly to investment opportunities with AI-powered analysis.
"You spend ₹16K eating out. Redirect ₹8K → this ELSS = ₹42L in 15 years"
02
India-Native by Design
Built for SIP, NPS, PPF, SGBs, NSE/BSE stocks, Gold ETF, and Indian real estate per-sqft data.
03
Real-Time AI Intelligence
Claude API for dynamic thesis generation — not static rule-based advice.
04
Behavioural Nudges
Gamified savings challenges tied to real investment milestones that drive action.
05
Regional Real Estate
Tier-2/3 city data: price/sqft, rental yield, appreciation trends — no competitor has this.
06
Income Benchmarking
Peer comparison by city and income bracket — know where you stand and how to grow.
"At 28 in Kanpur, your income should be ₹X — here's how to get there"

Target User Segments

Segment Income Range Core Need
Salaried Professional ₹30K – ₹2L/mo Wants to invest but doesn't know where to start. Primary segment.
Freelancer / Gig Worker Variable Irregular income — needs dynamic planning with variable surplus tracking.
Small Business Owner ₹1L+/mo Separate personal and business finance; reinvest profits intelligently.
Student / Early Career ₹20K – ₹60K/mo Building first investment habit. SIP-first approach, ages 22–27.
NRI / Returning Indian USD / Multi Invest in India — real estate + equity dual-track with currency management.
